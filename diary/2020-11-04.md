1. `Dynamic programming` is a technique for solving problems recursively. It can be implemented by memoization or tabulation. :algorithm:DP:
  * Tabulation(制表): Bottom Up. 
    通过列表存储结果供上层利用.
  * Memoization(记忆化): Top Down
    通过函数返回值递归调用.
